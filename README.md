# Customer Revenue & Retention Analysis

## Objective
Analyze customer revenue patterns and evaluate the impact of repeat vs non-repeat customers on business performance.

## Tools Used
- Excel (data understanding)
- SQL (data transformation & analysis)
- Power BI (dashboard & visualization)

## Key Insights
- Revenue is evenly distributed between repeat and non-repeat customers (~50/50)
- Monthly revenue trends remain stable across both segments
- No strong dependency on a single customer segment
- Average customer rating is 3.0 → indicates scope for improvement
